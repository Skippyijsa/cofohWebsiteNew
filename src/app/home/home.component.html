Here are the guests that are coming to COFOH!

The Colorado Festival of Horror (COFOH) 2023: Slasher Hotel annual event brings out all the slashers, ghouls and evil monsters on September 15-17. COFOH is the original, locally-owned, and fan-run convention, an intimate fan experience, creative incubator, and convention for all things horror. Our first Special Guest announcement is 1980s Scream Queen Michelle Bauer (“Nightmare Sisters”, “Sorority Babes in the Slimeball Bowl-O-Rama”) in her FIRST-EVER Colorado convention appearance!

Michelle Bauer was born and raised in Simi Valley, California. After an audition for director Fred Olen Ray, she began her horror feature film career with “The Tomb”, it would be the first of many. Michelle became one of the most prominent scream queens of the 1980s slasher films. She has been featured in “Evil Toons”, “Sorority Babes in the Slimeball Bowl-O-Rama”, “Nightmare Sisters”, “Hollywood Chainsaw Hookers” and many more. She has over 160 IMDb credits. Her life and career are one of the main subjects of the 2011 documentary “Screaming in High Heels: The Rise and Fall of the Scream Queen Era”. She returned for the 2020 follow-up “Screaming in High Heels: The Reunion” which reunited her with fellow actors and subjects Brinke Stevens and Linnea Quigley. Colorado Festival of Horror is excited to bring Michelle Bauer to Denver!

COFOH will host a variety of thrilling experiences and events throughout the weekend. The Death Dealers Room holds a menagerie of artists, authors, vendors, and horror comic book creators. Delightfully fiendish horror actors sell autographs, selfies, and merchandise, and participate in Q&As and film screenings. Wickedly good panels, workshops, and interactive audience events are all weekend long. This Dead Man’s Party is where it’s at!
